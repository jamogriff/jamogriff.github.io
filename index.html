My name is Jamison Griffith

I am a software engineer, multi-instrumentalist and dog dad residing in Colorado.

My interests are numerous, but the ones that really get me geeking out are music and technology.

Tech

From an early age I was captivated by technology and took apart every electronic device I could find. Today I practice that same fascination through software development. Whether it's consulting of a personal project, I'm typically cooking up something and I keep a worklog that documents my weekly goals and progress. It's a safe bet you can find me geeking out about software and more on Twitter and my tech blog.

Music

Piano jazz standards, acoustic blues, and heavy metal electric guitar riffs all feed my soul. I have a humble studio and when I have long stretches of free time I'll record and produce my own music.
