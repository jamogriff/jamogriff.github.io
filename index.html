My name is Jamison Griffith. I am a web developer, multi-instrumentalist and dog dad residing in Colorado.

My interests are numerous, but the ones that really get me excited are music and technology.

Tech

From an early age I was captivated by technology and took apart every electronic device I could find. Today I practice that same fascination through software development. Whether it's consulting or a personal project, I'm typically cooking up something and keep a worklog that documents my weekly progress. It's a safe bet you can find me geeking out about software and design on Twitter and my programming blog.

Music

Piano jazz standards, acoustic blues, and heavy metal electric guitar riffs all feed my soul. I have a humble studio and when I have long stretches of free time I'll record and produce my own music. Typically I don't have the patience for production, so I occasionally post unedited musical snippets on my YouTube channel.

Misc

I've always been fond of writing, but have not yet made a conscious effort to practice it consistently. My musings are a space to grow that skill more and posts may feature anything under the large umbrella of my interests.

Thanks for stopping by!
